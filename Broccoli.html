Here's some info about broccoli

Learning to garden can be difficult, especially when you live in an urbanized area. Luckily, we've gathered info to help you get started!

Broccoli is a great veg, useful in a lot of dinners.

When do I plant them?: The best time to sow Broccoli is mid-April, with the earliest being in February and the latest being the end of June.

How do I plant them?: Plant the seeds 1cm deep. After the seed has germinated, plant in a well-drained, rich, light to medium loam, and plant to the soil mark on the stem.
