Black beans

Learning to garden can be difficult, especially when you live in an urbanized area. Luckily, we've gathered info to help you get started!

Black beans are an easy to grow crop, and are a staple of mexican foods.

When do I plant them?: Black beans should be planted in late-Spring, so around April is best.

How do I plant them?: Black beans are grown from dried beans opposed to seeds. They're best suited to warm (20-27C), well drained soils, with a 6-6.5ph. Soak the dry beans overnight in clean, fresh water. If you want a continuous harvest, you shouldn plant "pole beans", but if you'd rather harvest them all at once plant a bush. Plant the beans 1 inch deep, with the eye facing down. Only water these when the soil is noticeably dry.
