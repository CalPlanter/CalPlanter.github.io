Mint

Learning to garden can be difficult, especially when you live in an urbanized area. Luckily, we've gathered info to help you get started!

Mint is a wonderful herb with a fresh flavour.

When should I plant it?: The best time to plant mint is in May.

How do I plant them?: Mint should be grown in a pot on its own since it grows quickly root-wise, and it should also be kept in sunlight. Its great to grow in a small container on the window sill in the kitchen, since it only takes 6-8 weeks to be ready to harvest.
