Carrots

Learning to garden can be difficult, especially when you live in an urbanized area. Luckily, we've gathered info to help you get started!

Carrots are a versataile vegetable, being used in soups, stews, and even a sunday roast

When should I plant it?: Spring, April is best.At the latest plant it in mid-May.

How do I plant them?: You should grow carrots in raised beds, with the seeds about 1cm apart in a rich, deep soil, 1cm down. Carrots can be harvested about 12 weeks after they're sowed, but they can also be harvested when they're 1-2cm in diameter
