Basil

Learning to garden can be difficult, especially when you live in an urbanized area. Luckily, we've gathered info to help you get started!

Basil is a wonderous herb that can liven up a dinner.

When do I plant them?: Basil should be planted in May, with the latest you can plant it being Mid-June.

How do I plant them?: Basil seeds should be planted 1cm deep in the soil. Once the seed has sprouted, plant the plant in a well drained rich light to medium loam. Plant to the soil mark on the stem.
