Heres the dirt on soil

Learning to garden can be difficult, especially when you live in an urbanized area. Luckily, we've gathered info to help you get started!

Many people struggle with information on the soil types, luckily we have the info you want!

Loam is often the best soil to use, preferably a mixture of 40% sand, 40% silt, and 20% clay, but other soils can work depending on the crop.

Luckily for us here in Ireland, most soil is a neutral PH, which is suitible for most plants. You can test your soil by buying a soil testing kit, which are availible at most garden centers. If your soil is alkaline (over 7 on the PH scale or blue on a litmus strip), you can change this by adding sulfur or gypsum, if it is acidic however (below 7 on the PH scale or red on a litmus strip), you can solve this by adding woodash or lime.
