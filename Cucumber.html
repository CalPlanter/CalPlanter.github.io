Cucumber

Learning to garden can be difficult, especially when you live in an urbanized area. Luckily, we've gathered info to help you get started!

Cucumbers are a useful vegetable, and go perfect in a sandwhich or whatever else you may need

When should I plant it?: Cucumber should be planted in April if indoors, or May if outdoors.

How do I plant them?: Plant the seeds 1cm deep in a small pot, and wait for sprouts to shoot. After they've sprouted, plant in rich, deep and well drained soil, with lots of dung/compost.
